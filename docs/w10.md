## What I did in ACIT 2811 (UX/UI)

### TASK User Testing
- bring your wireframes on paper
- all group members arrive on time please
- the wireframes should be indicative of your actual final redesign
    should contain:
    - actual content (headings) - to give users context when testing
    - actual links 

- during user testing you CANNOT talk to the tester (except to indicate when something wrong has happened)
- during the user testing you are only observing (taking notes on any unexpected things/actions the users do)
- in groups > 1, one person can be the 'facilitator' (switching pages in and out) and 1 can be the 'observer' (taking notes)

### How did we do it

- Our group have 3 people so we decide to switch role (facilicator, observer and tester) after each test.
- After finish testing, everyone in class and also Crish gave us their opinions and what change to make it better.
- We note them down to use it for later.
