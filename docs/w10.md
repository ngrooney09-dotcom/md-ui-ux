## What I did in ACIT 2811 (UX/UI)

### TASK User Testing

- Bring your wireframes on paper  
- All group members must arrive on time  
- Wireframes should represent your final redesign and include:
    * Actual content (headings) to give users context  
    * Actual links  

- During user testing:
    * You cannot talk to the tester (except to guide tasks)  
    * You are only observing and taking notes  
    * In groups, one person acts as the facilitator (switching pages if needed)  

### How did we do it

- Our group have 3 people so we decide to switch role (facilicator, observer and tester) after each test.
- After finish testing, everyone in class and also Crish gave us their opinions and what change to make it better.
- We note them down to use it for later.
