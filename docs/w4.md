## Problem Statements

### Clarify the 5 Ws

**Five Ws**
* Who           Who is using the site 
* What          What is the site offering/doing,what should the main focus be
* Where         Online
* When          ASAP, later?
* Why           Why create/redesign the site

## Information Architecture (IA)

Organizing, labelling of content - to help users find information 

**Comprised of several 'systems':**
- navigation (what type of menu, how many menus, hierarchy)
    - hierarchy 
        deep (very few top menu links with a lot of links underneath)
        shallow (many top menu links with very few links underneath)
    - labelling (what style of language do we use)
    - content inventory (listing all of the pages/content that the website)
    - sitemap (a visual representation of all the content)
    - wireframes (simple line drawings depicting the layout of menus/pages)
